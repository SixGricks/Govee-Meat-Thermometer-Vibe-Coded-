# Govee BBQ Alarms

Turn your Govee multi-probe BBQ thermometer (already in Home Assistant via the
Govee BLE integration + a Bluetooth proxy) into a full alarm system — named
probes, high/low targets, an "approaching target" pre-alert, push
notifications to your phone with a **Pause** button and 5-minute reminders,
and a dashboard card to drive it all.

**Setup is all UI** — no YAML:

1. Install via HACS, then restart Home Assistant.
2. **Settings → Devices & Services → Add Integration → "Govee BBQ Alarms"**.
3. Pick your probe temperature sensors and the phone(s) to notify.
4. Add the **Govee BBQ Card** to a dashboard (it auto-detects your device).

See the [README](https://github.com/SixGricks/Govee-Meat-Thermometer-Vibe-Coded-) for full
details.
